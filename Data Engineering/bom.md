Bill of Materials

1. LM393 Comparator IC
Reference: IC1
Quantity: 1
Description: Dual voltage comparator used to compare the input voltage with the reference voltage.

2. Resistor R1
Value: 10 kΩ
Quantity: 1
Description: Used as part of a voltage divider to create the reference voltage.

3. Resistor R2
Value: 10 kΩ
Quantity: 1
Description: Works with R1 to form the voltage divider network.

4. Resistor R3
Value: 10 kΩ
Quantity: 1
Description: Pull-up resistor used for the comparator output.

5. Resistor R4
Value: 220 Ω – 1 kΩ
Quantity: 1
Description: Current limiting resistor for the LED.
6. Resistor R5
Value: 220 Ω – 1 kΩ
Quantity: 1
Description: Current limiting resistor for the second LED indicator.

7. Potentiometer (RV1)
Value: 10 kΩ
Quantity: 1
Description: Used to adjust the reference voltage and set the threshold level.

8. LED D1
Type: Green LED
Quantity: 1
Description: Indicates normal voltage condition.

9. LED D2
Type: Red LED
Quantity: 1
Description: Indicates when the voltage crosses the threshold level.

10. Connector (J1)
Type: 2-Pin Connector
Quantity: 1
Description: Used to connect the power supply to the circuit.

11. Power Supply
Voltage: 5V – 9V DC
Quantity: 1
Description: Provides power to the comparator circuit.
